Class Info

This course is broken up into five three-week-long sessions each ending with an exam. In order to pass a session, you'll need to accumulate a minimum amount of experience (XP). Earn XP by completing Challenges – exercises to be completed on your schedule, with your own resources, and using your own brain.

Tracks

Due to the fact that "web design" is more of an umbrella term for many various disciplines and good web designers need many different skillsets, this course will offer three Tracks: Development, Visual Design, and User Interaction. Students will choose a Track at the beginning of the semester. This choice will influence how grades are applied to Challenges.
